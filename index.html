Our Heritage

A Tradition of Excellence

Welcome to Calicut Stories, where every dish is a chapter in the rich culinary history of the Malabar coast. We bring the authentic, time-honored recipes of our ancestors right to your table, emphasizing traditional roots and a deep connection to Malabari culture.

As a proud venture of the Brokadan Group of Companies, we are committed to uncompromising quality and absolute authenticity. Our dedication ensures that every dining experience is a journey through the vibrant flavors and aromatic spices of the region, carrying the warmth of our heritage.
